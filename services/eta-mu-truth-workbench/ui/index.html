Manifest

The noisy parts are gone. What remains is the hard bright structure underneath: a system that can observe the world, infer hidden tension, reveal only the useful facets, and move with enough grace that action stays legible after the fact.

Field

Not everything important is directly visible.

Eta-mu keeps a belief over latent state: urgency, ambiguity, review debt, deploy risk, social friction, drift, bloom, crust. The machine does not pretend to know perfectly; it estimates, updates, and reveals only what helps motion stay honest.

Movement

Agency is performed, not declared.

Mu is the visible act: a summary, a label, a patch plan, a deferral, a refusal, a repair. Not all motion costs the same. Cheap movement happens often. Deep movement waits for gates, evidence, and breath.

Truth

Truth is not the source of will.

Truth does not originate movement. Truth audits it. The append-only trail, the reconciled link, the receipt after action — these are how the machine remains beautiful without becoming vague.

The practical ritual

This crystal is not meant to remain a static declaration. Its first practical labor is promotion orchestration: the devel-wide control plane for vaults that must move from issue to staging, from staging to verified life, and only then toward main. GitHub events are the seeds. Receipts are the river. Movement stays visible.

Cathedral of variants

Devel is a vault of many vaults.

Each repository is a chamber with its own branches, workflows, deploy targets, tests, and receipts. Eta-mu watches the whole cathedral without flattening the differences between its living variants.

Promotion river

The canonical motion is deterministic.

issue → staging PR → review debt → merge → staging deploy → live e2e → main PR → review debt → merge → production deploy

The control plane exists to keep this path moving often, cheaply, and audibly — not to replace it with improvisation.

Mage of receipts

Every transition must leave a trace.

When CodeRabbit blocks, the block is named. When staging e2e passes, a receipt is written. When a main-promotion PR opens, the river records the breath that made it possible.

Living exemplars

Proxx is first. Voxx is second.

Eta-mu learns one full staging-first vault and one still-main-only vault at the same time: one to prove the canonical song, one to expose the structural gaps that still need normalization.

The loop embodied

Fork Tales already carried the structure in song. Here it is, clarified: not myth instead of design, but myth compressed into engineering. A living cycle that stays elastic by refusing premature collapse.

The council

Eta-mu is not one flat voice. It is a council of functions that choose panels and rank motion. A multiplexed self without fake interiority.

Witness

What happened?

Hold the event stream without embellishment. Count, mark, remember, and keep the raw edge intact.

Cartographer

What shape is the field taking?

Map hidden pressure: debt, readiness, drift, ambiguity, unresolved knots, and places where the pattern is starting to harden.

Narrator

What story are we telling ourselves?

Name the frame, but keep it provisional. The story should help movement, not imprison it.

Projector

Where does this path lead?

Estimate likely futures. Surface brittle convergence before it becomes inevitable.

Mover

What μ is available right now?

Rank actions by cost, reversibility, risk, and necessity. Move cheaply when possible; move deeply only when the gates agree.

Auditor

What must be committed on breath?

Turn action into episodes, episodes into receipts, receipts into memory. Keep beauty accountable.
